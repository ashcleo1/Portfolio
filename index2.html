I'm a creative producer and studio operations leader with 8+ years running integrated campaigns from concept through delivery for brands like Disney, StartEngine, and Los Cabos Tourism Board.

My work lives at the intersection of creative direction and production management. I've led studios of 16+ people, managed agency and vendor networks across video, digital, social, CTV, and experiential, and built the intake systems, workback schedules, and QA frameworks that keep high-volume, high-stakes creative work running without breaking.

I started at MMGY Global in luxury travel marketing, moved into entertainment at OMG23 managing Disney campaigns with budgets exceeding $5M each, then spent four years as Director of Creative Services at StartEngine building and running a 16+ person integrated studio. Today I run BICHROME, my creative consultancy.

I don't just move work along. I raise the standard of what comes out the other side.

Behind
the Work

Great creative doesn't happen by accident — it happens because someone built the infrastructure.

01

Intake & Briefing

Every project starts with a clear brief. I build intake systems and brief templates that capture goals, audience, messaging, and constraints before a single asset goes into production.

02

Workback Scheduling

I map every project backwards from the delivery date — identifying dependencies, flagging risks early, and keeping all teams and partners aligned to milestones.

03

Agency & Vendor Management

I've managed networks of 16+ staff, contractors, and agency partners simultaneously — setting expectations, giving clear direction, and holding people accountable.

04

QA & Brand Standards

Nothing ships without passing through a quality check. I build QA frameworks that catch misalignment, spec errors, and consistency issues before they reach the client.
